About Us

Learn more about our church, our mission, and our vision

Our Story

The Supernatural Assembly (TSA) was founded in 2013 by Pastor Seyi Kolawole and Tara Kolawole.

TSA is a Word-based church built on the pillars of love, prosperity, soul-winning, teaching, healing, the Holy Spirit, and the demonstration of God's power. Our mission is to transform lives through the undiluted Word of God, empowering believers to walk in faith, purpose, and divine authority.

Our Mission

To lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ by creating environments where people are encouraged and equipped to pursue intimacy with God, community with insiders, and influence with outsiders.

Our Vision

To be a church that demonstrates the love and power of God, transforms lives, builds strong families, and impacts our community and the world with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Core Values

Biblical Authority

We believe the Bible is God's Word and the ultimate authority for our lives and ministry.

Spirit-Led Worship

We value authentic, Spirit-led worship that creates an atmosphere for God's presence.

Community

We believe spiritual growth happens best in the context of meaningful relationships.

Service

We are committed to serving others as an expression of God's love.

Outreach

We are passionate about sharing the Gospel locally and globally.

Generosity

We believe in living generously with our time, talents, and resources.

Our Pastors

Pastor Kolawole

Lead Pastor

Pastor Seyi has been leading The Supernatural Assembly since its founding in 2013. He is deeply passionate about transforming lives through the power of God's Word, equipping believers to walk in faith and purpose.

He is also a prolific author, with works including: Speaking Confidently in Tongues and How Satan Influences Lives.

Pastor Tara Kolawole

Lead Pastor

Pastor Tara Kolawole co-leads The Supernatural Assembly, bringing deep spiritual insight and a heart for nurturing believers into their divine purpose. She is passionate about empowering individuals, especially women, to embrace their God-given identity and live victoriously.

She is also a celebrated author, with impactful books such as The Proverbs 31 Woman, Sozo, Wield the Sword, and Daily Bread.
