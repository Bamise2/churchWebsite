Why We Give

At The Supernatural Assembly, we believe that giving is an act of worship and an expression of our faith. When we give, we partner with God in His work and demonstrate our trust in His provision.

Our Bible verse, 2 Corinthians 9:8, reminds us: " And God is able to make all grace abound toward you, that you, always having all sufficiency in all things, may have an abundance for every good work."
